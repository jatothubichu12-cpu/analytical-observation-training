# What is Analytical Observation?

Observation = Attention + Curiosity + Thinking + Pattern Recognition

## Fact vs Assumption
- Fact = What you directly see or hear
- Assumption = What you think it means

## 5 Step Formula
1. What do I SEE?
2. What do I HEAR?
3. What is the MOOD?
4. What is NOT normal?
5. What does it MEAN?
